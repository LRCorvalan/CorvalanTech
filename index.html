Arc Flash Hazard Analysis

Incident energy calculations, arc flash labels, PPE recommendations, and practical mitigation options for electrical equipment.

Short Circuit Studies

Fault current calculations used to evaluate equipment ratings and support protective device selection.

Coordination Studies

Protective device settings and time-current curve evaluations to improve selectivity and reduce nuisance outages.

NFPA 70E Support

Electrical safety guidance, energized work practices, shock boundaries, and field-focused documentation.

SKM PowerTools Modeling

Power system modeling, study updates, device settings, and report preparation using SKM PowerTools.

Power System Analysis

Load flow, motor starting, grounding, harmonics, and related studies depending on project needs.
