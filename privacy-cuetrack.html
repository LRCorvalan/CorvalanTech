CueTrack Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 31, 2025

CueTrack respects your privacy. The app is designed to be simple and transparent.

Information We Collect

  • No names, emails, or phone numbers are required.
  • No location data is collected.
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used.
  • No advertising networks are integrated.

How Your Data Is Used

All game data, including scores, stats, and history, is stored locally on your device. The information does not leave your phone unless you choose to back it up using iCloud or your device backup system.

Third-Party Services

CueTrack does not integrate with or send data to third-party services.
