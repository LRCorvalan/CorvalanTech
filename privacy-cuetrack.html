CueTrack – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 8/31/2025

CueTrack respects your privacy. This app is designed to be simple and transparent.

Information We Collect

CueTrack does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

  • No names, emails, or phone numbers are required.
  • No location data is collected.
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used.
  • No advertising networks are integrated.

How Your Data Is Used

All game data, including scores, stats, and history, is stored locally on your device. This information never leaves your phone unless you choose to back it up using iCloud or your device’s backup system.

Third-Party Services

CueTrack does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services.

Children’s Privacy

CueTrack is safe for all ages. It does not contain adult content, nor does it request or collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever make changes to this Privacy Policy, the updated version will be posted within the app and on our website.
