CueTrack Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 8/31/2025

CueTrack respects your privacy. The app is designed to be simple and transparent.

Information We Collect

CueTrack does not collect, store, or share any personal information. No names, emails, phone numbers, location data, analytics, tracking tools, or advertising networks are used.

How Your Data Is Used

Game data such as scores, stats, and history is stored locally on your device. This information never leaves your phone unless you choose to back it up using iCloud or your device backup system.

Third-Party Services

CueTrack does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services.

Children’s Privacy

CueTrack is safe for all ages. It does not contain adult content and does not request or collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If changes are made to this policy, the updated version will be posted within the app and on this website.
