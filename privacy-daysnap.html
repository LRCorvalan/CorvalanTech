DaySnap Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2, 2026

DaySnap values your privacy. Videos and app data are stored locally on your device. DaySnap does not collect personal information, track users, or store content on external servers.

Information We Collect

DaySnap does not collect names, emails, phone numbers, analytics, advertising identifiers, or personal content.

How Your Data Is Used

Your clips are used only inside the app to display your timeline and create compiled videos.

Third-Party Services

If you choose to share a video, sharing is handled by Apple’s built-in system tools.
