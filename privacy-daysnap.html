DaySnap Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2/02/2026

DaySnap values your privacy.

Information We Collect

Videos and data are stored locally on your device. DaySnap does not collect personal information, track users, or store content on external servers.

How Your Data Is Used

Your clips are used only to display your timeline and create compiled videos within the app. When you choose to share a video, it is shared using Apple’s built-in system tools.

Deleting Data

If you delete a clip, it is permanently removed from your device.

Contact

For questions, use the contact form on the CorvalanTech website.
