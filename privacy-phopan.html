Phopan – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 9/1/2025

Phopan respects your privacy. This app is designed to help you manage and document completed panels without collecting or sharing personal information.

Information We Collect

Phopan does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

  • No account creation is required.
  • No names, emails, or contact details are collected.
  • No analytics or advertising tools are used.

How Your Data Is Used

  • Photos you take and tasks you complete are stored locally on your device.
  • Completed tasks are shown within the app only.
  • If you choose to share data via iCloud, Dropbox, or other services, those files are handled directly by those platforms. Phopan does not access them.

Third-Party Services

Phopan does not use or connect to any third-party services. If you choose to share data with services like iCloud or Dropbox, that sharing is managed entirely by iOS and the selected service. Phopan has no involvement.

Children’s Privacy

Phopan does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for all users.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, the new version will be posted within the app and on our website.
