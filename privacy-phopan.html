Phopan Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 9/1/2025

Phopan respects your privacy. This app is designed to help you manage and document completed panels without collecting or sharing personal information.

Information We Collect

Phopan does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. No account creation is required. No names, emails, or contact details are collected. No analytics or advertising tools are used.

How Your Data Is Used

Photos you take and tasks you complete are stored locally on your device. Completed tasks are shown within the app only. If you choose to share files through services like iCloud or Dropbox, that sharing is handled by those platforms.

Third-Party Services

Phopan does not use or connect to any third-party services. If you choose to share data with another service, that sharing is managed by iOS and the selected service.

Children’s Privacy

Phopan does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the new version will be posted within the app and on this website.
